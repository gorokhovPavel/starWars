Venator

Venator — the Republic’s main star destroyer during the Clone Wars, combining powerful weapons with a large starfighter hangar. Acclamator — an assault ship used to transport clone troopers, vehicles, and equipment to battlefields across the galaxy. Senator — a diplomatic vessel designed for transporting senators and carrying out important missions for the Galactic Republic.

Death Star

Death Star — a massive battle station built by the Galactic Empire, equipped with a superlaser capable of destroying entire planets. It was designed to spread fear and secure Imperial control across the galaxy.

Aclamotor

Acclamator-class Assault Ship — a large Republic transport ship used to carry clone troopers, vehicles, and supplies across the galaxy. It played a key role in deploying Grand Army of the Republic forces during the Clone Wars.

MC100 Star Cruiser — a powerful capital ship used by the Resistance, featuring strong shields, heavy armament, and advanced command systems. It served as one of the Resistance fleet’s main flagships during the war against the First Order.

Imperial I-class Star Destroyer

Imperial Star Destroyer — the main warship of the Galactic Empire, designed to enforce Imperial rule through overwhelming firepower, heavy armor, and a powerful military presence across the galaxy.

Resurgent-class Star Destroyer

Resurgent-class Star Destroyer — the primary warship of the First Order, larger and more powerful than the Imperial Star Destroyers of the Galactic Empire. It carried advanced weapons, powerful shields, and a large complement of troops and starfighters.

War next to Lothal

The Battle of Lothal was an important victory for the Rebel Alliance. Grand Admiral Thrawn attacked the planet Lothal with the Imperial fleet. Ezra Bridger used the help of the purrgil, giant space creatures, to defeat the Empire. The purrgil took Thrawn and Ezra into hyperspace, allowing the Rebels to win. After the battle, Lothal was finally free from the Galactic Empire.

Armored Assault Tank Mk I

The Armored Assault Tank Mark I (AAT) was a battle tank used by the Trade Federation during the Clone Wars. It was heavily armed with a powerful main cannon and laser weapons. The AAT was strong, fast, and effective in ground battles against enemy troops and vehicles.
